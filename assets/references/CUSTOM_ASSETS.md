# Custom assets Downtown needs that have not been mentioned

Five opinions. Ranked by leverage. Effort estimates are in hours
or days of a competent designer, not calendar time.

---

## 1. Favicon and app icon (must-have before launch)

The full stacked wordmark will not read at 16px or 32px (browser
tab, bookmark, phone home screen). At those sizes the bars and
type collapse into noise. Downtown needs a custom mark for small
sizes.

**Recommendation:** a single-bar version of the symmetry trick.
A black square with the letter `D` knockout in Cream, rotated 180
on the bottom half so the icon also reads upside-down. The same
rotational gag, compressed to one glyph.

**Deliverables:**
- 16, 32, 48, 64, 128, 192, 512px PNG
- One SVG that scales
- Apple touch icon (180px, rounded square)
- Open Graph image (1200x630px) using the full wordmark and the
  marquee strip

**Effort:** half a day of a designer who already understands the
brand. One day if they are coming in cold.

**Why this is must-have:** every tab, every share, every mobile
bookmark uses this. Shipping without it looks unfinished.

---

## 2. Weekly programme card and Saturday flyer template (highest leverage)

The brand IS the programme. A weekly Saturday flyer is the
single most-shared asset Downtown will produce. Without a
template, every flyer is a fresh design decision and the brand
drifts within months.

**Recommendation:** a Figma template (free, multi-user, Vin can
edit) with three variants:

- **Square (1080x1080).** Instagram feed and reposts.
- **Story (1080x1920).** Instagram and TikTok story.
- **Landscape (1920x1080).** Site hero card and WhatsApp share.

Each variant pre-fills with the Big Shoulders Display heading,
the date strip, the Bonfire accent block, and slots for up to
four artist names plus a one-line note (doors time, cover, special).

**Deliverables:** a single Figma file with the three frames and
locked styles. A README inside the file explaining what to change
and what to leave alone.

**Effort:** one day of a designer plus a 30-minute walkthrough
with Vin or whoever runs the weekly post.

**Why this is high-leverage:** every Saturday for the next five
years uses this. Compounding return.

---

## 3. Wordmark variants beyond the current stacked mark (already flagged in DESIGN.md)

DESIGN.md section 2 already names these. Restating here because
they are real deliverables that need commissioning:

- **Clean SVG of the existing stacked mark.** The JPG in
  `assets/logos/DT_Logo.jpg` is a raster proof and will pixellate
  on retina and at scale. Required for shipping.
- **White-on-Ink variant.** Needed for dark sections. Can be
  derived from the SVG.
- **Horizontal single-bar lockup.** For places the stacked mark
  does not fit (footer bar, narrow social headers, email signature).
  Reads `DOWNTOWN` left to right with no flip. Lower priority,
  but worth having before the site goes wide.

**Effort:** half a day total if all three are produced together.

---

## 4. Social media post template set (medium leverage)

Instagram is the primary site CTA. The feed should not be more
polished than the site. A template set of five or six post
formats covers most of what Downtown will ever post:

- **Tonight card.** What's on, doors, cover, in Big Shoulders +
  Bonfire accent.
- **This weekend card.** Friday and Saturday side by side.
- **Artist announce.** Single artist name, big, with a date and
  the venue mark.
- **Aftermovie still.** Photograph plus a Big Shoulders caption.
- **Closed Mondays card.** Recurring filler that reinforces the
  voice ("Monday we sleep.").
- **Pinned: about Downtown.** The one-paragraph site description
  in poster form.

**Deliverables:** the same Figma file as the flyer template, with
these added as extra frames.

**Effort:** half a day added to the flyer-template work.

**Why this is medium not high:** the site's primary CTA is
"follow on Instagram," but Vin already runs an Instagram. If the
existing feed has a working visual language, this is enhancement,
not foundation. If the feed is currently inconsistent, promote
this to high leverage.

---

## 5. Motion wordmark for Reels and venue kiosks (long-tail)

A two to three second animation of the wordmark performing its
rotational flip: stacked, top bar rotates into place from offscreen,
bottom bar rotates in from below, settles into the final mark.
Plays at the start of every Reel or TikTok and could also play
silently on a small screen at the venue entrance.

**Deliverables:** an MP4 and a transparent-background WebM,
1080x1080 and 1080x1920.

**Effort:** one day from a designer who is comfortable in After
Effects or Rive.

**Why long-tail:** valuable but not blocking anything. Ship the
site and the flyer template first. Come back to this once weekly
programming is humming.

---

## Suggested order of commissioning

If budget is one batch:

1. Clean SVG wordmark (must-have, blocks production).
2. White-on-Ink and horizontal variants (must-have, half day extra).
3. Favicon and OG image (must-have for launch).
4. Weekly flyer + IG template set (highest ongoing leverage).
5. Motion wordmark (after launch, once weekly cadence is running).

A single competent designer can produce 1 through 4 in a week,
fixed-scope. Worth getting all four done together so the system is
internally consistent.
